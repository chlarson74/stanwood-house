This beautifully illustrated version of Proverbs 31 makes a perfect gift for a wedding, anniversary, birthday, Mother's day, Valentine's Day, bat mitzvah, or any special occasion. Each verse is illuminated with one of seven original borders by Judaica artist Yonnah Ben Levy, who also designed a special Hebrew calligraphy. Her husband Chaim Bezalel, depicts only her hands in such activities as knitting, gardening, ceramics, painting, and simply celebrating life. There is a special dedication page for the giver to fill out with the recipient's name.
