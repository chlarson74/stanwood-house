Aviv (Spring) Wildflowers of Israel – 8 signed limited edition prints on Whatman 100% cotton rag paper

Landscapes and flowers of Israel..Each print is 8 x 10 in., signed and numbered. Portfolio of 8 - $75.

Portfolio made of handmade paper by Israeli paper maker, Natan Kaaren. (Each portfolio is unique because each handmade paper is unique.



Chaim Bezalel and Yonnah Ben Levy, a husband and wife collaboration began their work together in Israel in 1989. These prints were produced through a unique process, combining photography and painting. They have exhibited their work in Israel and the United States, including several museum shows. Many of their larger works have been commissioned or purchased for public venues.
